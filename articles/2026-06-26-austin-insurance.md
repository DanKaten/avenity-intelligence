## Overview

We analyzed 10 insurance agencies in Austin, Texas to assess their current AI search visibility — specifically, their ability to appear when potential customers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews questions like *"who is the best insurance agent in Austin?"*

Austin's rapidly growing population, large tech and startup workforce, active homebuyer market, and significant inbound migration from high-cost states create strong personal and commercial insurance demand. Austin buyers also skew younger and more tech-savvy — making them early adopters of AI search for insurance comparisons. This report identifies where each business stands on AI search presence.
