## Overview

We analyzed 10 mortgage brokers and lenders in Austin, Texas to assess their current AI search visibility — specifically, their ability to appear when potential customers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews questions like *"who is the best mortgage broker in Austin?"*

Austin's historically hot real estate market, high home prices relative to Texas averages, large tech buyer population with complex income situations (RSUs, stock options, 1099 income), and active investor market create distinctive mortgage demand. Austin buyers are among the most research-heavy in Texas — and AI search is a primary research tool. This report identifies where each business stands on AI search presence.
