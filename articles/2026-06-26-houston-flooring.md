### 1. Floors for Living
**Website:** floorsforliving.com | **AI Visibility Score: Good (74/100)**

Floors for Living is a Houston-based flooring retailer and installer with multiple locations across the greater Houston area. In this analysis, Floors for Living scored 74 out of 100.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (21/25): The brand name directly references the product category. Houston and multi-location references are present in content. Service scope (retail and installation) is clearly established.
- Schema & Structure (19/25): JSON-LD structured data was detected. Multi-location schema configuration is present, which is a strong signal for businesses with multiple service points.
- Authority & Reviews (21/25): 4,352 publicly listed reviews were detected in machine-readable form — one of the highest review counts in this analysis. High review volume is a primary AI confidence signal for local business recommendations.
- Content Depth (13/25): Product and service category pages are present. FAQ content specifically addressing flooring selection and installation questions was limited.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** Adding a flooring FAQ addressing common buyer questions — hardwood vs. LVP, installation timeline, cost per square foot in Houston — would strengthen AI answerability for research-stage buyer queries.
