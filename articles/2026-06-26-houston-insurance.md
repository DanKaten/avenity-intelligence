## Overview

We analyzed 10 independent insurance agencies in Houston, Texas to assess their current AI search visibility — specifically, their ability to appear when potential customers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews questions like *"who is the best insurance agency in Houston?"*

Houston's insurance market is highly competitive, with strong demand across personal, commercial, home, and flood insurance segments. This report identifies where each agency currently stands on AI search presence and what technical factors affect their visibility.

### 1. Texan Insurance
**Website:** texaninsurance.com | **AI Visibility Score: Good (76/100)**

Texan Insurance is a full-service, family-owned independent insurance agency that has been serving Texas since 1985, with a strong Houston market presence. In this analysis, Texan Insurance scored 76 out of 100.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (22/25): "Texan Insurance" is a strong brand signal that connects to the state. The 1985 founding date and family-owned positioning are referenced prominently. Houston and Texas service scope are clearly established.
- Schema & Structure (19/25): JSON-LD structured data was detected. The schema foundation supports AI engine categorization.
- Authority & Reviews (21/25): 40+ year operating tenure is a significant AI credibility signal. Review data in machine-readable form was partially present.
- Content Depth (14/25): Personal and commercial insurance service pages are present. FAQ-format content addressing common insurance buyer questions was limited.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** Adding FAQ content covering common Houston insurance questions — flood coverage, home insurance costs in Harris County, auto insurance requirements — would directly strengthen AI answerability for high-intent buyer queries in the Houston market.
