Texas United Mortgage is a Houston-based mortgage lender providing home purchase loans, refinancing, and home equity solutions with a focus on competitive rates and fast closings. In this analysis, Texas United Mortgage scored 77 out of 100.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (22/25): "Texas United Mortgage" directly signals state and service category. Houston base and mortgage service scope are clearly established in primary content.
