## Houston Moving AI Visibility Findings

### 1. Ameritex Movers
**Website:** ameritexmovers.com | **AI Visibility Score: Good (83/100)**

Ameritex Movers is a Houston-based moving company providing local, long-distance, and commercial moving services throughout the metro and beyond. In this analysis, Ameritex Movers scored 83 out of 100.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (23/25): The H1 directly establishes Houston moving as the primary service. The service scope — local, long-distance, commercial — is clearly defined in primary content. City and category alignment is strong.
- Schema & Structure (21/25): JSON-LD structured data was detected. Service-level markup is present across the site. The schema foundation is among the more complete in this analysis.
- Authority & Reviews (22/25): 2,815 publicly listed reviews were detected in machine-readable form — among the highest in this review group. Review volume at this level is a primary AI confidence signal for local service recommendations.
- Content Depth (17/25): FAQ-format content was detected. Service-specific pages cover local, long-distance, and commercial categories. Additional neighborhood-specific pages would further extend geographic depth.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** Adding apartment-specific or neighborhood-specific moving pages targeting Houston's largest residential districts would capture additional long-tail AI query matches.
