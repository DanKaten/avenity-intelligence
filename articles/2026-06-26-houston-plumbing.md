## Houston Plumbing AI Visibility Findings

### 1. Nick's Plumbing
**Website:** nicksplumbing.com | **AI Visibility Score: Good (72/100)**

Nick's Plumbing has been serving Houston residents and businesses since 1979, making it one of the city's longest-running plumbing services. In this analysis, Nick's Plumbing scored 72 out of 100.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (21/25): The brand name, service category, and Houston location are all clearly established in primary page content. The longevity claim (since 1979) functions as an AI-readable authority signal.
- Schema & Structure (18/25): JSON-LD structured data was detected. The schema foundation supports AI engine categorization. Service-level markup on individual service pages could be expanded.
- Authority & Reviews (20/25): FAQ-format content was detected on the site. Plumbing-specific FAQs are a strong AI visibility signal because they match the question format that AI engines use to generate direct answers.
- Content Depth (13/25): Service pages are present. Additional location-specific service pages covering Houston neighborhoods would strengthen geographic signal depth.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** Adding neighborhood-specific service area pages (Katy, The Woodlands, Sugar Land) would extend the geographic signal and help AI engines match Nick's Plumbing to location-specific queries.
