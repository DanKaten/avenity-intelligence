## Houston Roofing AI Visibility Findings

### 1. Rose Roofing
**Website:** roseroofing.com | **AI Visibility Score: Good (79/100)**

Rose Roofing has served Houston as a roofing company since 1972, working with homeowners, property managers, and real estate professionals across the metro. In this analysis, Rose Roofing scored 79 out of 100.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (22/25): The H1 directly states "#1 Houston's Roofing Company Since 1972." City, category, and tenure are all front-loaded. This is one of the strongest entity clarity statements in this review.
- Schema & Structure (19/25): JSON-LD structured data was detected in the page source. The schema foundation is solid, though service-level markup on individual service pages could be strengthened.
- Authority & Reviews (22/25): 267 publicly listed reviews were detected in machine-readable form on the homepage — a meaningful authority signal. Review schema presence strengthens AI engines' ability to read and cite this data.
- Content Depth (16/25): FAQ-format content was detected on the site. Additional service-specific pages and location-based content would further strengthen depth signals.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** Expanding FAQ content to cover storm damage, insurance claims, and roof replacement timelines — common buyer questions in the Houston market — would further strengthen content depth scores.
