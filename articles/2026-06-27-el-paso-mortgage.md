## Overview

We analyzed 10 mortgage businesses in El Paso, Texas to assess their current AI search visibility — specifically, their ability to appear when potential customers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews questions like *"best mortgage lender in El Paso"* or *"VA loan lender near me El Paso."*

El Paso's mortgage market has unique dynamics — VA loans represent a disproportionately large share of originations due to Fort Bliss, the market has strong first-time homebuyer demand, and local credit unions play a significant role. At an average origination value of $8,000, the mortgage market has the highest per-transaction value of any service category we track.
