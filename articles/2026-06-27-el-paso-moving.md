## Overview

We analyzed 10 moving businesses in El Paso, Texas to assess their current AI search visibility — specifically, their ability to appear when potential customers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews questions like *"best moving company in El Paso"* or *"local movers near me El Paso."*

El Paso's moving market has distinct drivers — military personnel relocations from Fort Bliss, cross-border moves between El Paso and Ciudad Juárez, and strong inbound migration from other Texas cities. At an average job value of $2,800, the moving market rewards businesses that capture buyer attention at the moment of search — which is increasingly an AI-driven moment.
