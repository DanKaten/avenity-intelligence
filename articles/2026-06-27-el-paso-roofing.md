## Overview

We analyzed 10 roofing businesses in El Paso, Texas to assess their current AI search visibility — specifically, their ability to appear when potential customers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews questions like *"best roofing company in El Paso"* or *"roof replacement near me El Paso."*

At an average job value of $9,000, the El Paso roofing market has the highest per-job revenue of any category analyzed. AI search visibility at this price point is especially impactful.
