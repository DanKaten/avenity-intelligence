## Houston HVAC AI Visibility Findings

### 1. Houk Air Conditioning Houston
**Website:** houkac.com | **AI Visibility Score: Strong (90/100)**

Houk Air Conditioning Houston serves the greater Houston metro as a dedicated residential and commercial HVAC provider. In this analysis, Houk Air Conditioning Houston scored 90 out of 100 — the top score in this report.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (25/25): The homepage clearly identifies as a Houston HVAC company with a specific service statement and geographic references throughout. Title tag, H1, and meta description are well-aligned.
- Schema & Structure (25/25): Four distinct structured data blocks were detected, including LocalBusiness and Service schema. NAP data is present and consistent. This is the strongest schema implementation in this analysis.
- Authority & Reviews (15/25): Review signals are present on the site. Review schema markup was not detected, which limits AI engines' ability to read rating data directly from the page without navigating to third-party platforms.
- Content Depth (25/25): FAQ content is present on service pages, giving AI engines direct-answer material. Multiple service-specific pages were detected.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** Adding review schema markup (AggregateRating) to service pages would allow AI engines to surface the star rating and review count directly in AI-generated answers — completing an otherwise strong technical setup.
