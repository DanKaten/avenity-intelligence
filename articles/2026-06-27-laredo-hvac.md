## Overview

We analyzed 10 HVAC businesses in Laredo, Texas to assess their current AI search visibility — specifically, their ability to appear when potential customers ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews questions like *"who is the best HVAC company in Laredo?"*
