## Overview

We analyzed 10 insurance agencies in Laredo, Texas to assess their current AI search visibility. Laredo's insurance market is shaped by its unique position at the US-Mexico border: trucking and logistics insurance, cross-border commercial coverage, and bilingual service are competitive differentiators that don't exist in most US markets. As buyers increasingly use AI tools to find and vet insurance agencies before making contact, digital visibility has become a front-of-funnel differentiator.
