Each business is assessed across four technical dimensions using publicly observable signals only:

### 1. Golden Grove Cleaning
**Website:** goldengrovecleaning.com | **AI Visibility Score: Strong (86/100)**

Golden Grove Cleaning provides commercial and janitorial cleaning across Conroe, including office cleaning, medical office sanitization, warehouse cleaning, and floor care. In this analysis, Golden Grove Cleaning scored 86 out of 100.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (22/25): The homepage title anchors both "Conroe TX" and "Janitorial Services," and the page carries an unusually high density of Conroe-specific location signals. AI engines can readily identify what the business does and where it operates.
- Schema & Structure (24/25): Multiple structured-data blocks were detected, including both CleaningService and LocalBusiness markup plus aggregateRating data. This is the most complete structured-data foundation in this analysis — it lets AI engines parse the entity, its services, and its rating signals directly.
- Authority & Reviews (19/25): aggregateRating markup and publicly visible review content were both detected, giving AI engines a machine-readable satisfaction signal.
- Content Depth (21/25): Extensive FAQ-format content and multiple service-level pages were detected — one of the deepest content footprints in this report.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** With structure and content already strong, the next lever is expanding neighborhood-level service pages across Montgomery County (Willis, Montgomery, The Woodlands) to match more location-specific AI queries.
