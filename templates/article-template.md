## Houston HVAC AI Visibility Findings

### 1. Abacus Plumbing, Air Conditioning & Electrical
**Website:** abacusplumbing.com | **AI Visibility Score: Good (72/100)**

Abacus Plumbing, Air Conditioning & Electrical is one of Houston's larger multi-trade service providers with a strong local track record. Abacus Plumbing, Air Conditioning & Electrical scored 72 out of 100 — the strongest score in this analysis.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (20/25): The homepage clearly identifies as a Houston residential service company. Primary service emphasis shifts between plumbing and HVAC across different pages, which can create some ambiguity for AI parsing of the primary category.
- Schema & Structure (18/25): LocalBusiness schema is present. Service-level schema was not detected on individual HVAC pages. NAP is consistent across major platforms.
- Authority & Reviews (19/25): 2,400+ publicly listed Google reviews at 4.7 stars. Review schema markup was not detected on the website, which limits AI engines' ability to read this rating data directly from the page.
- Content Depth (15/25): Strong homepage and main service pages. FAQ-format content addressing common AI-asked questions ("how long does AC installation take," "what size unit do I need") was not found on service pages.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** Adding structured FAQ content to service pages would give AI engines direct-answer material to cite, increasing the likelihood of appearing in conversational search results.

### 2. One Hour Air Conditioning & Heating of Houston
**Website:** onehourhvac.com/houston | **AI Visibility Score: Developing+ (61/100)**

One Hour Air Conditioning & Heating of Houston operates as a franchise location serving the greater Houston area. One Hour Air Conditioning & Heating of Houston scored 61 out of 100 in this analysis.

**Technical findings:**
- Entity Clarity (16/25): The franchise structure means the Houston location shares a domain with the national brand. This can reduce local entity signals since AI engines may associate the domain with the national organization rather than the Houston-specific location.
- Schema & Structure (15/25): National-level schema is present but not localized to the Houston location. Local business markup specific to the Houston address was not detected.
- Authority & Reviews (18/25): 800+ publicly listed local reviews. Brand recognition is strong. Review velocity appears active.
- Content Depth (12/25): Service page content is templated at the national level rather than written for Houston-specific queries or local climate context.

**Highest-impact opportunity:** Adding Houston-specific local business schema and locally-written service page content would strengthen the location-specific signals AI engines use to connect this business to Houston searches.
